Hi, I'm Adam Jacques.

I'm a designer & creative developer based in Manchester.

Currently working at DCG as Multimedia designer.

In this role I create engaging multimedia experieces, on the web, in-store and in print. I love to identify opportunities for innovation, productivity, usability, accessibility and visual improvements to create experiences and interfaces that people love to use. I also really enjoy project management, problem solving, sketching, brainstorming, playing with new technologies & hackathons.

My skillset includes...

  • Web design & development
  • Motion / CSS Animation
  • Iterative design
  • Video editing & post prodcution
  • Project management

One of my current goals is to focus my attention on UX & Product design. I really enjoy iterative design, collaborating on projects with designers & discovering new ways of delivering experiences to users. I'm currently looking for roles which can challenge & harden my design skills, ideally within the creative, start-up or charitable sectors.

I'm massively inspired by the work of Ivan Zhao at Notion, the working methodologies of Cal Newport, design studios Zero & 1/1, the packaging designers CPGD.

Once upon a time, I was a self employed screen printer, working with one69a and running a label called Ishii prints. During this time, I worked with Umbro, Monkey Shoulder, Dr Dre Beats, Raw Design, Nike Training Club & JP74.
